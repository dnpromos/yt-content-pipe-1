Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

1. Introduction

clipmatic.video ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our software and services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Information: Email address used for purchase and license activation.
  • Payment Information: Processed securely by our third-party payment provider. We do not store credit card details.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous usage statistics to improve our product (can be disabled).

3. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect, store, or have access to your API keys or secrets.
  • We do not collect, store, or process the content of your generated videos, scripts, images, or audio.
  • All AI processing happens directly between your machine and the AI providers using your own API keys.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver and activate your software license.
  • To send product updates and important notices.
  • To provide customer support when you contact us.

5. Third-Party Services

Our software connects to third-party AI providers (e.g., Wiro, ElevenLabs, OpenAI) using your own API keys. Your use of those services is governed by their respective privacy policies. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for their practices.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. Your API keys are stored locally on your machine and are never transmitted to our servers.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your license is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of non-essential communications.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via email or through our website.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@clipmatic.video.
