Questions & Answers

What do I need to run this?

Python 3.11+, Node.js 18+, and API keys for AI generation and voice synthesis. Everything runs locally on your machine.

Do I need to pay a subscription?

No. You pay $64.99 once for the app and get lifetime access with free monthly updates. The only ongoing cost is what you spend on AI API calls — and that goes directly to the providers, not us.

How much does each video cost to produce?

A typical 5-section video with AI-generated images costs around $1-2. With AI video clips, it's roughly $3-5 depending on clip count and duration. The built-in cost calculator shows you the exact breakdown before you generate.

Can I customize the output?

Absolutely. You can edit narration, swap image prompts, upload your own images, change voice, adjust transitions, resolution, caption styles, and more — all through the web UI or by modifying the source code directly.

What AI providers does it support?

Multiple AI providers for LLM, image gen, video gen, and speech-to-text (Whisper for captions), plus ElevenLabs-compatible voices. New providers are added with each monthly update.

What kind of videos can I make?

Listicle-style videos — "Top 10 AI Tools", "5 Best Travel Destinations", etc. Output in landscape (16:9) for YouTube or portrait (9:16) for TikTok, Shorts, and Reels. All videos include auto-generated TikTok-style captions with customizable styling.
