Questions & Answers

What do I need to run this?

A Mac or Windows PC, and API keys for AI generation and voice synthesis. Everything runs locally on your machine.

Do I need to pay a subscription?

No. You pay $64.99 once for the app and get lifetime access with free monthly updates. The only ongoing cost is what you spend on AI API calls — and that goes directly to the providers, not us.

How much does each video cost to produce?

A typical 5-section video with AI images costs $1-2. With AI video clips, roughly $3-5 depending on clip count and duration. The built-in cost calculator shows the exact breakdown.

Can I customize the output?

Absolutely. Edit narration, swap image prompts, upload your own images, change voice, adjust transitions, resolution, caption styles, and more.

What AI providers does it support?

Multiple providers for LLM, image gen, video gen, and speech-to-text (Whisper for captions), plus ElevenLabs-compatible voices. New providers added with each update.

What kind of videos can I make?

Listicle-style videos — "Top 10 AI Tools", "5 Best Destinations", etc. Landscape (16:9) for YouTube or portrait (9:16) for TikTok, Shorts, and Reels. All include auto-generated TikTok-style captions.
